Site Plan

Welcome to the site plan page.

Site Name: Flavorful Creations

The name "Flavorful Creations" was selected to reflect the joy of cooking and experimenting with different flavors. It is engaging, easy to remember, and represents both recipes and cooking tools.

Site Purpose:

This website aims to provide users with a fun and informative platform for exploring cooking. It will include a variety of recipes, information on essential cooking tools, and engaging cooking-related content to inspire home cooks of all skill levels.

Scenarios:

"I want to find a quick and easy recipe for dinner tonight."

The recipes page will offer categorized recipes, including quick and easy meals.

"What are the must-have kitchen tools for a beginner?"

The cooking tools page will provide a guide on essential kitchen tools and their uses.

Color Schema:

Warm Orange (#FFA500) - Used for headings and accent elements to evoke a sense of warmth and appetite.

Soft Beige (#F5F5DC) - Used as the background color to provide a neutral and inviting feel.

Typography:

Lobster - Used for headings to create an inviting and stylish look.

Open Sans - Used for body text to ensure readability and a clean, modern appearance.

Wireframe:

Home Page: Fun cooking tidbits, featured recipes, and a navigation menu.

Recipes Page: A categorized list of recipes.

Cooking Tools Page: A showcase of essential kitchen tools with descriptions.

CSS:

A basic CSS file named project.css will be created to implement the selected color schema and typography.
